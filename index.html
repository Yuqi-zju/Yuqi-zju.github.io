Biography

My name is Yuqi Zhao (赵宇骐).

I am a Bachelor(Eng.) student in Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University, majoring in Opto-Electronics Information and Science. I am a researcher in SING@ZJU advised by Prof. Daoxin Dai and also a full-time exchange researcher in LPQM@EPFL, advised by Prof. Tobias Kippenberg. My current research interest surrounds photonics device design and optical mechanics, with a focus on inverse design methodologies, optical frequency combs, and electrical-optical-mechanical interactions.

I am looking for Ph.D. position at 2024 fall. You can find my CV here.
